Who is qualified as an influencer ?

An influencer is typically someone who has a significant following or presence on social media platforms and possesses the ability to impact the opinions, behaviours, and purchasing decisions of their audience.

  1. Reach and audience size: Minimum 10K Followers
  2. Expertise: Travel related, food, travelling, cycling

Vetting process

Vetting influencers is to ensure your values, content, and audience alignment with our brand and goal.

  • Review content
  • Assess engagement and audience quality
  • Evaluate online presence
  • Performance tracking and measurement
